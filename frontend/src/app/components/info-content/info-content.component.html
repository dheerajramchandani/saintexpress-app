This portal is a dedicated web interface for the Significance Analysis of INTeractome (SAINTexpress) tool. It simplifies the transition from raw proteomics data to high-confidence results by providing a point-and-click environment for scoring protein-protein interactions.

About SAINTexpress

SAINTexpress is an industry-standard statistical tool used in Affinity Purification-Mass Spectrometry (AP-MS) research. In these experiments, it is often difficult to distinguish a true biological "partner" protein from common laboratory contaminants or background noise.

As detailed in the original research publication, the software uses a sophisticated mathematical model to compare your experimental samples against negative controls. It assigns a probability score to each interaction, allowing you to focus your research on the most likely biological candidates.

How to Use the Portal

The system is designed to take you from upload to results in four simple steps:

  • Initialize a Session: Create a secure workspace for your specific experiment.
  • Upload Input Files: Provide your three standard data files. Detailed formatting instructions can be found in the SAINTexpress Manual.
    • Interaction File: Maps which preys were found with which baits.
    • Prey File: Lists the proteins identified in your mass spec.
    • Bait File: Defines your experimental baits and control groups.
  • Select Your Method: Choose the scoring mode that matches your data:
    • SPC (Spectral Counts): Best for data based on the number of identified spectra.
    • INT (Intensity): Best for data based on continuous MS1 intensity values.
  • Run & Download: Start the analysis with one click. The portal manages the execution and provides a final, scored text file for you to download.

Data Management & Server Maintenance

To ensure the portal remains fast and available for all researchers, we practice a "clean desk" policy:

  • Download your Results: Once the process is complete, download your output file immediately to your local computer.
  • Delete Session: After you have successfully saved your results, click the Delete Session button. This permanently removes your input and output files from our server, freeing up valuable storage space for other users.

Key Benefits

  • No Technical Setup: You do not need to download the source files, install compilers, or manage complex software libraries.
  • Automated Error Checking: The portal checks for missing files before starting and provides clear feedback if the analysis encounters an issue, helping you troubleshoot your data formatting quickly.
  • Accessibility: Perform high-level statistical filtering from any computer with a web browser, regardless of your operating system.

security Privacy & Data Policy

Your privacy is paramount. Data uploaded to this portal is used exclusively for your specific analysis session. The developers do not access, view, or use your data for any other purpose.

Users are responsible for clicking Delete Session after downloading results to clear server space. As a secondary measure, all session data is automatically purged from our servers every 24 hours.
